oracle.exe

Oracle

Multi-agent reasoning engine

Agentic AI · 2025

A council of debating agents, a skeptic, an optimist, and a fact-checker, each one running on a different frontier model, argue over your data until they reach consensus, then hand back the answer with a confidence score drawn straight from where they disagreed.

STACK

    ↳ How it fitsEach agent is a LangGraph node wired to a different model, Claude, GPT, Gemini, and Grok, so they genuinely disagree; a moderator node weighs the arguments and turns the spread of opinion into the confidence score, so the disagreement is the metric.

    switchboard.call

    Switchboard

    AI receptionist that answers the phone

    Voice AI · Twilio · 2025

    An AI voice agent that picks up real phone calls, talks to the caller in natural speech, works out what they need, and books them straight onto a live calendar. No hold music, no phone tag. It runs on a real Twilio number, so to the caller it is just someone answering the phone.

    STACK

      ↳ How it fitsTwilio streams the call audio in, Whisper turns it into text, an agent decides what to say and checks the calendar for open slots, then speaks back and writes the booking, all fast enough to feel like a normal conversation.

      churn.ipynb

      Churn Radar

      Telecom customer churn prediction

      Machine learning · UNT · 2022

      A model that flags the telecom customers most likely to cancel before they actually do, trained on real subscriber data and tuned to surface the handful of churn signals that genuinely move the needle. Built at the University of North Texas.

      STACK

        ↳ How it fitsI cleaned and explored the subscriber data in pandas, engineered the contract and usage features that carried the most signal, then put logistic regression up against gradient-boosted trees. The feature importances doubled as a story about why people leave.

        helmsman.yaml

        Helmsman

        Agentic SRE copilot

        Kubernetes · 2025

        An on-call agent that watches your cluster, reads the logs and metrics the moment something breaks, explains the likely root cause in plain English, and drafts the kubectl/Helm fix for a human to approve.

          cue.live

          Cue

          Real-time answer copilot for calls

          Voice AI · 2025

          A quiet overlay that listens to your call and reads your screen, then feeds you the answer, the number, or the next line to say while you are still talking. Same idea as Cluely: always on top, visible only to you, and it actually follows the conversation instead of just transcribing it.

          STACK

            ↳ How it fitsIt captures the call audio and a view of your screen, transcribes locally with almost no lag, and streams the context to a model that drafts what to say next, all rendered in an always-on-top window that only you can see.

            Infra · 2024
