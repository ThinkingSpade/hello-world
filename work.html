oracle.exe
O

Oracle

Multi-agent reasoning engine

Agentic AI · 2025

A council of debating agents — a skeptic, an optimist, and a fact-checker — argue over your data until they reach consensus, then hand back the answer with a confidence score drawn straight from where they disagreed.

STACK

    ↳ How it fitsEach agent is a LangGraph node with its own persona prompt; a moderator node weighs their arguments and turns the spread of opinions into the confidence score the React front end renders — so the disagreement is the metric.

    querybird.sql

    Querybird

    Conversational analytics agent

    Text-to-SQL · 2024

    Ask your warehouse anything in plain English. Querybird writes the SQL, runs it, walks you through its reasoning step by step — and is honest enough to tell you when the data simply can't answer the question.

    STACK

      ↳ How it fitsA LangChain planner reads the dbt schema to draft SQL, runs it on Snowflake in a read-only sandbox, then streams both the rows and a step-by-step rationale back to Next.js — the schema is what keeps the agent honest.

      houston-pulse.live
      H

      Houston Pulse

      Real-time civic dashboard

      Open data · 2024

      A live “city mood” index that fuses Houston's open data — traffic, air quality, and 311 calls — into a single heartbeat for the city, re-scored every minute.

      STACK

        ↳ How it fitsAirflow pulls each open-data feed on a one-minute cadence and streams it through Kafka into DuckDB; a scoring job blends the signals into one index that Plotly and Mapbox render as a live, zoomable city map.

        cashflow.map

        Cashflow Cartographer

        Data-storytelling tool

        NLP · Fintech · 2023

        Drop in a year of messy transactions and get back an interactive map of where your money actually went — narrated in plain English by an agent that has read every single line.

        STACK

          ↳ How it fitsPlaid imports the transactions, Pandas categorizes and clusters them, an LLM writes a plain-English story for each cluster, and D3 lays the whole year out as an interactive Sankey of where the money flowed.
