## Overview

How Cascadia Distribution Co. executes a product recall across PDX-1, RNO-1, and CMH-1: lot traceability in Ledger, inventory holds, customer notification waves, reverse logistics, and regulator communication. Recall coordinator: Reyna M.

Severity classes: Class I (safety risk, regulator-driven), Class II (possible harm), Class III (quality only). A mock recall runs annually against the Ledger sandbox; targets are 100% lot traceability within 2 hours and 100% customer notification within 72 hours.

## Preconditions

- Recall notice validated with the supplier (for example, Brightway Electronics quality bulletin with affected lot list) or generated by internal QA.
- Recall ticket opened (format `RC-2024-011`) and coordinator assigned.
- Affected lot numbers confirmed in writing; never run a recall on a verbal lot list.
- Conference bridge and recall channel opened; legal looped in for any Class I.
- Coordinator holds Ledger roles `INV_HOLD_ALL` and `TRACE_READ`.

## Steps

1. **Trace the lots.** Establish where every unit is: on hand, in transit, or shipped.

   ```sql
   -- On hand by DC and location
   SELECT l.lot_id, l.sku, i.dc, i.location_id, i.qty_on_hand
   FROM ledger.lots l JOIN ledger.inventory i USING (lot_id)
   WHERE l.lot_id IN ('LOT-BW-24187', 'LOT-BW-24188');

   -- Shipped in the trailing 12 months
   SELECT s.order_id, s.customer_id, s.ship_date, sl.qty_shipped
   FROM ledger.shipments s JOIN ledger.shipment_lines sl USING (shipment_id)
   WHERE sl.lot_id IN ('LOT-BW-24187', 'LOT-BW-24188');
   ```

2. **Hold inventory in every DC.** Holds block allocation, picking, and adjustment in one step:

   ```bash
   ledger inventory hold --lot LOT-BW-24187 --reason RECALL --scope all-dcs --ticket RC-2024-011
   ```

3. **Stop outbound movement.** Cancel open waves containing the lots, intercept packed-not-shipped cartons on the dock, and physically move held product to the quarantine cage with `RECALL` placards. In-transit shipments: request carrier intercept through Jordan P.

4. **Notify customers in waves.** Wave 1 (0-4 hours): customers with product in transit. Wave 2 (4-24 hours): customers who received the lots in the last 90 days. Wave 3 (24-72 hours): all remaining consignees on the trace.

   ```text
   Subject: URGENT - Product Recall RC-2024-011 / SKU BW-CHG-0450 / Lots LOT-BW-24187-88
   Action required: quarantine listed lots immediately and stop sale/use.
   Reply with on-hand quantity by lot within 24 hours.
   Return instructions and credit terms follow in the RMA packet.
   ```

5. **Run reverse logistics.** Issue an RMA per customer, book return freight on BlueRidge Freight, and route all returns to the CMH-1 quarantine cage only. Returned units are received against the recall ticket, never into sellable stock.

6. **Communicate with the regulator (Class I and II).** The coordinator drafts, legal reviews, and one named signatory sends. Class I: initial report within 24 hours of confirmation, then status updates every 48 hours until close.

7. **Disposition recovered product.** Destroy with a witness and certificate of destruction, or return to vendor per the supplier agreement. Record every disposition against the recall ticket.

8. **Close out.** Compute the effectiveness rate (units recovered or confirmed destroyed vs. units traced), hold the lessons-learned review, and archive the ticket with all correspondence.

## Verification

- Mass balance closes on every lot: produced equals on-hand held plus recovered plus destroyed plus customer-confirmed:

  ```sql
  SELECT lot_id, qty_received, qty_held, qty_recovered, qty_destroyed, qty_customer_confirmed
  FROM ledger.recall_reconciliation
  WHERE ticket = 'RC-2024-011';
  ```

- Available-to-promise for every affected lot is zero in all three DCs.
- Notification log shows 100% of traced customers contacted, with timestamps inside the wave windows.

## Rollback

- If the recall is rescinded or its scope narrows, release holds only with coordinator approval and the supplier's written retraction attached to the ticket:

  ```bash
  ledger inventory release --lot LOT-BW-24188 --ticket RC-2024-011 --approver reyna.m
  ```

- Send a correction notice to every customer already contacted; a rescinded recall is never closed silently.
- Mock recalls run entirely with `--env sandbox`; production holds are never used for drills.

## Escalation

1. Reyna M. (coordinator) is the single decision point; all status flows through the recall channel.
2. Legal for every regulator contact and any Class I decision.
3. Supplier quality (Brightway Electronics, Guadalajara Metalworks, or Meko Textiles) for scope questions; Jordan P. for intercepts and return-freight capacity.
