## What the diagram shows

The three-DC network: PDX-1 in Portland is the import gateway receiving
ocean freight from Hangzhou and Ho Chi Minh via Pacific Lane (22-38 day
transits); Guadalajara and Monterrey feed CMH-1 by truck. A daily FTL
transfer lane links PDX-1 to CMH-1 (4.1-day average), RNO-1 handles
cold-chain and West overflow via a shuttle from PDX-1. 68% of import volume
lands at PDX-1 and 71% of customers sit within 2-day ground coverage.

## How to read it during a disruption

If PDX-1 or the ocean lanes are impaired (port congestion, customs holds),
the whole network feels it within a week - check the Shipments 2024 dataset
for lane status and see the Network strategy review for the Southeast
coverage gap that amplifies East-bound delays.
