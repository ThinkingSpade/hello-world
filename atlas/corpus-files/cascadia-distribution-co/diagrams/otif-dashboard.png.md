## What the dashboard shows

Trailing six-month on-time-in-full by supplier against the 95% target line.
Domestic suppliers (Cascade Ridge, Willamette Kitchenworks, Monterrey
Fasteners) clear the bar; Tundra Fresh sits just under it. Brightway
Electronics (93.1) and Meko Textiles (91.7) miss on ocean-lane variability,
Shenzhen Corelight (88.9) is a new supplier on probation, and Guadalajara
Metalworks (84.2) reflects the 2024 restructuring (see: the supplier
insolvency disruption report). Full numbers: Supplier scorecard dataset.

## How to read it during sourcing decisions

Anything under target either carries a risk flag in the scorecard or gets
one at the quarterly review; single-sourced SKUs from a below-target
supplier are the first candidates for the dual-sourcing rule.
